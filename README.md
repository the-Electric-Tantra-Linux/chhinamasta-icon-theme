# chhinamasta-icon-theme

> Ta Devi Sarva Bhutesa Krodha Rupena Samshita 
> Namestasyai Namestasyai Namestasyai Namo Namaha

<small><i>roughly translated</i>We bow to the Divine Goddess existing in all, who resides in the form of Anger. We bow to Her, we bow to Her and we continually bow to Her.</small>

<h4>- Mantra to the Goddess Chhinamasta </h4>



<h1>About the Icon Theme</h1>


Its basically Darcwaita + Places & Mimetypes from Winter, with some modifications to various icons as was necessary to make them look right within the intended implementation, use on 3D buttons drawn with awesomewm's cairo surface.
